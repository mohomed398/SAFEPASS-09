Privacy Policy — SafePass

1. Introduction

SafePass ("the Service", "we", "our") is a local, browser-based password generator. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use the Service.

2. No Passwords Are Collected

All passwords are generated locally inside your browser. We do not transmit, store, log, or share any passwords you generate. Nothing is sent to our servers (because none are used).

3. Local Storage

The Service may store non-sensitive settings (such as selected options or theme) in your browser's localStorage so that your preferences are remembered. These settings never leave your device.

4. No Tracking

SafePass does not use:

  • cookies
  • analytics scripts
  • tracking pixels
  • user profiling

We do not track your identity, behavior, IP address, or device information.

5. Third-party Links

The Service may link to third-party websites or tools. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external sites. Always review third-party policies before interacting with them.

6. Security

Since all password generation occurs locally, the security of your device and browser directly affects the safety of passwords you generate. Use up-to-date software and avoid insecure environments.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

8. Contact
